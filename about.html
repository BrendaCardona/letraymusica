Letra y Musica

What is Letra y Música?

Letra y Música, (Poetry & Music), is a local artistic movement encouraging poets, songwriters, musicians, actors, visual artists and bohemians gather once a month at Taboo Lounge Gallerie to share ideas and have a good time. The event is inspired by Latin American ‘Peñas’, where artists gathered to express and share ideas back in the 60s. ‘Peña’ is a cultural community center where local folk art is exhibited to the public. ‘Peña’ is where creativity seeds grow and make social transformation possible. Letra y Música started in De Tapas Restaurant, (Spanian restaurant in Addison, TX) in May, 2005, with 100 artists involved..

Brief Story

Founder: Josué Gabriel de Montemayor
Manifest: The impact of Spanish Literature, along with Latin American music in the United States of America, has exploded in recent years, mainly due to the growth of Spanish speakers in the country. Due to this increase in the Hispanic population, and with the spirit and the need to create new platforms for the promotion and Literary and Musical education, which lead to founding new cultural spaces, the idea of ​​holding this festive and sometimes furtively spontaneous meeting was forged. , between Literature and Music. At the same time, we seek to integrate the Spanish language among the different American cultures, and nurture each other. In each monthly event, it is intended to make a celebration of Art and Culture, with absolute freedom and without ties to any religion, political ideology, racism or xenophobia, practicing sexual diversity and universal art, all thanks and, for the love of art .
For Letra y Música Dallas, innumerable poets and singer-songwriters, duets, trios, groups, ensembles and even mariachis have performed. With all kinds of musical genres. Small plays, monologues, performance, declamation, stand up comedy, slam poetry, and other artistic manifestations.
It started as a simple gathering of artists. Over time it transformed from being a bohemian night, with frequent performances by artists from the local Dallas-Fort Worth scene, to being the launching pad for new Latin American voices. Including artists from other cities in Texas, from other states, and over time, from other countries.
Desde su inicio en 2005, caminó y creció de la mano de la Feria Internacional del Libro de Dallas, hasta 2011. El fundador de LyM Dallas, fue miembro de la mesa directiva de dicha Feria durante ese tiempo.
For several years, radio and television programs were produced for Letra y Música Dallas, broadcast on local networks such as Univision, Telemundo and Azteca América, among others.
This 2016, Lyrics and Music Dallas returns, with the presentation of the novel Inhóspito, by the founder of the event, Josué Gabriel de Montemayor, and a special presentation by the Mexican singer-songwriter, Gabriel Flores, in addition to other presentations by local poets and singer-songwriters.
